About Us

         At Caraga State University-Main Campus, we are committed to providing a world-class educational experience for our students, faculty, and staff. We understand that the quality of campus facilities and services plays a pivotal role in shaping this experience. To uphold our commitment to excellence, we have introduced the UniVoice: CSU Student Feedback Portal, an internally developed initiative that places the voice of our campus community at the heart of our decision-making process.

Our Mission

        To continually enhance the quality of services and facilities within our institution. We believe that the best way to achieve this goal is by actively seeking feedback from those who matter most – you, the members of our campus community.


We invite you to be an active participant in this journey of improvement. Your feedback is not just a suggestion – it's a powerful tool for making a difference in the quality of your educational experience. Together, we can create a campus environment that not only meets but exceeds your expectations.

Thank you for being a part of our commitment to excellence in campus facilities and services. Your voice matters, and your input is the catalyst for positive change.
